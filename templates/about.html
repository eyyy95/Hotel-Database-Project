Hotel of Hotels Database - About

This site offers a way for countless travelers to travel across the world with less barriers to find a room that fits their budget and size requirements. This database was designed as the foundation of a travel lodge booking web app, where customers can search for lodging/hotel rooms in certain locations and place orders/make reservations. Both customers and hotels can keep track of customer information, orders, and reserved rooms. This idea was developed by Samuel Leon, with Ed Yeow providing assistance in app/database development.
